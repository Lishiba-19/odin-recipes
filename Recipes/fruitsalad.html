Fruit Salad

A fruit salad for the entire family.

This is an easy, refreshing strawberry and blueberry salad recipe that can be enjoyed all year long.

Ingredients

  • 1 pint strawberries, hulled and quartered
  • 1 pint blueberries
  • Â½ cup white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 4 bananas

Steps

  1. Combine strawberries and blueberries in a large bowl. Sprinkle with sugar and lemon juice; toss lightly. Refrigerate until cold, at least 30 minutes.
  2. About 30 minutes before serving, cut bananas into 3/4-inch-thick slices; toss with berries until evenly distributed.
